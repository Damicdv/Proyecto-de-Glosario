About the Project

The glossary project originated with a simple yet fundamental goal: to create a reliable and accessible source of programming terms and concepts that anyone can quickly consult at any time. By building this glossary as a website, we aim to take advantage of the benefits of online accessibility, allowing users to find the information they need with ease, from any device with an internet connection.

The glossary is exclusively focused on programming, a field that features multiple terminologies and concepts that can often be complex for those who are learning or working in the area. Through this platform, we aim to primarily support students, although anyone who needs a quick reference can benefit. We know that remembering all the technical details and terms is not always easy, especially when one is in the middle of a project or intensive learning process. That's why our intention is for the glossary to serve as a sort of "digital companion" for the user, where they can always find help.
